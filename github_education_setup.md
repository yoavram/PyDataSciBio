# Setting Up GitHub Copilot Pro (Student Plan)

As a student, you're eligible for **GitHub Copilot Pro for free** through the GitHub Student Developer Pack. This gives you unlimited completions and access to the most capable Copilot models — a significant upgrade from the free tier you used in the first class.

Follow the steps below to claim it. Note that **approval can take anywhere from a few minutes to a couple of days**, so start this early.

---

## Step 1: Create or Log In to Your GitHub Account

If you signed in to Copilot during the first class, you already have an account — go straight to Step 2.

Otherwise, go to [github.com](https://github.com) and create a free account. Use your **university email address** if possible, as it speeds up verification significantly.

---

## Step 2: Apply for the GitHub Student Developer Pack

1. Go to [education.github.com/students](https://education.github.com/students).
2. Click **Sign up for Student Developer Pack**.
3. Sign in with your GitHub account if prompted.
4. Fill in the application:
   - Select **Student** as your role.
   - Enter your **university name**.
   - Use your **university email address** (e.g., `yourname@tau.ac.il`). This is the fastest path to approval.
5. If your university email isn't recognized automatically, you'll be asked to upload **proof of enrollment** — a photo of your student ID or an official enrollment letter works well.
6. Submit the application.

> **Tip:** Applications submitted with a university email address are usually approved within minutes. Uploads requiring manual review may take 1–2 days.

---

## Step 3: Confirm Your Approval

You'll receive an email from GitHub once your application is approved. You can also check your status at any time by visiting [education.github.com](https://education.github.com) and signing in.

Once approved, your account will be upgraded to **GitHub Pro**, and **GitHub Copilot Pro** will be automatically enabled at no cost.

---

## Step 4: Verify Copilot Pro Is Active in VS Code

1. Open VS Code.
2. Click the **Copilot icon** in the bottom status bar.
3. You should see the title changed to **Copilot Pro Usage**.
